In India crisis situation caused due to disaster is very common in India. Cyclones & Tsunami are common around the 5600 km coastline in India as per records. Apart from that nearly 75-80% of Indian sub-continent is prone to cyclone. Earthquake has recently created threats in many parts of the country especially in the northern, eastern & north-eastern belt of India. In such situation there is a much needed approach to respond to the emergencies by helping hand with food, organising relief camps, & providing knowledge regarding the preparedness training towards disaster management programmes be it from the local bodies or from the national fronts.
